Privacy Policy for CyberOptix

Introduction

CyberOptix a student-led initiative under the Myanmar Institute of Information Technology (MIIT), operates the e-commerce platform www.cyberoptix.mm. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information while ensuring transparency and compliance with academic and ethical standards. By using our website, you agree to these terms.

Information We Collect

We collect the following data to serve you effectively:


  1. Personal Information : Name, email, phone number, and shipping address during account creation or checkout.Student ID (optional) for academic discount verification.
  2. Transaction Data: Payment details (processed securely via PayPal; we do not store credit card information).Order history and product preferences.
  3. Technical DataIP address, browser type, device information, and cookies (see Section 7).Website usage patterns via Google Analytics.

How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  1. Process orders and deliver products.
  2. Provide customer support (e.g., tracking shipments, resolving issues).
  3. Send transactional emails (order confirmations, delivery updates).
  4. Improve our platform through anonymized usage analytics.
  5. Conduct academic research (aggregated, non-identifiable data only).

Data Sharing

We only share data under these circumstances:
Third-Party Services: Payment processors (PayPal), logistics partners (e.g., delivery companies), and MIIT supervisors (for academic review).
Legal Compliance: If required by Myanmar authorities under applicable law.
Business Transfers:In case of mergers or acquisitions (with user consent).

Data Security

We implement:

  1. SSL encryption for all data transfers.
  2. Regular security audits supervised by MIIT’s IT department.
  3. Limited access to personal data (only essential team members).

Your Rights

You may:
Access/Correct Data: Update your profile via account settings.
Delete Account: Request deletion by emailing [support@cyberoptix.mm].
Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in each message.


Cookies

We use:

  1. Essential Cookies: For login sessions and cart functionality.
  2. Analytical Cookies: To improve site performance (opt-out via browser settings).
  3. Marketing Cookies: For personalized ads (disabled by default).

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Policy Changes

Updates will be posted here 30 days before taking effect. Continued use implies acceptance.
