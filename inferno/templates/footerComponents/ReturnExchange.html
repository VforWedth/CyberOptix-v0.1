Return & Exchange Policy for CyberOptix

Eligibility for Returns/Exchanges

We accept returns or exchanges under these conditions:

  1. Timeframe:Request must be made within 7 days of delivery.
  2. Condition:Unused, undamaged, and in original packaging.Tags/labels intact.
  3. Proof:Provide order number or receipt.

Return Process

  • Step 1: Notify us via:

    Email: [returns@cyberoptix.mm]
    Phone: [+95 9 123 456 789]

  • Step 2: Pack the item securely with the original invoice.

  • Step 3: Ship to:

    CyberOptix Returns MIIT Campus, Mandalay Myanmar
    Note: Return shipping costs are the customer’s responsibility unless the error is ours (e.g., wrong item shipped).

Refund Policy

Approved Returns: Refund processed within 3-5 business days via the original payment method.
Exceptions: Refunds exclude return shipping fees.Damaged/missing items may require investigation (up to 7 days).

Exchanges

Same Product: Free exchange if stock is available.
Different Product: Price difference applies.
Out-of-Stock Items: We’ll issue store credit or refund.

Non-Returnable Items

Opened software/licenses.
Perishable items (e.g., laptop cleaning kits).
Custom-configured devices.

Damaged/Defective Items

Report Within 24 Hours: Email photos/video to [support@cyberoptix.mm].
Resolution: Free replacement or full refund.

Academic Project Disclaimer

Returns may take longer during exam periods or university closures.
All decisions are reviewed by MIIT academic supervisors for fairness.
